What Is A Gold Loan?

A gold loan is a loan against one's owned gold. Individuals need to pledge their gold ornaments to a Bank or non-banking financial institution, based on which the lender would extend funds to the owner. In most cases, borrowers would acquire only a certain percentage of the gold's worth, known as the loan-to-value (LTV).

What are the things to keep in mind when taking a gold loan?

As far as urgent funds or emergency loans are concerned, as long as you have the collateral, a gold loan is your best bet. Is a CIBIL score required for a gold loan?

CIBIL scores aren't always required while you are applying for a loan. One of the several perks of subscribing to an online gold loan is that it does not require a stellar credit history. The borrower's credit score is not an integral element of the eligibility criteria. All one requires to avail of a gold loan is collateral and a few basic KYC documents. The said basic documents comprise of basic identity and address proof. Sometimes, lenders even approve loans just based on the Aadhar Card. On the other hand, in the case of an unsecured loan, the borrower has to submit their CIBIL score as a mark of their financial health.
